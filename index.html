Projects

Off Hue Go

A game made in the course Computer Game Design, we did the whole game from scratch, where my main focus was the graphics and the final report.

Prostate Geovis

A geographical visualization of prostate cancer patients in Stockholm county.
The project was made as a part of the course Information Visualization and was nomitated in the C awards in the category "Bästa forskningspotential".


Pokedex

A game made in the course Interaction Programming and the Dynamic Web. I was responsible for storing the data in firebase and also worked on the overall design of the project.

FieldDay

An application made in the course Mobile development and Web Technologies where I was responsible for the login and general design of the application. The point of the application is to see where your friends are at the moment and chat with them if needed.


Hueniverse

A VR game made in the course Advanced Graphics and Interaction where the player is supposed to avoid or destroy the colored asteroid heading for the player. I was responsible for implementing the gameplay and the movement of the ship.

Amazeballs

An AR game made in the course Advanced Graphics and Interaction where I was responsible for the gameplay and implementing the AR for the game. The game is based on the analogue labyrinth game and the goal is to get the ball to the finish line by cooperating with two other friends with the help of their mobile phones. The game was also nominated to the C Awards in the category "Nästa publikmagnet".
